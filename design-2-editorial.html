Reading Preview — Post Typography

Moving personal infrastructure to a new platform is always a mix of excitement and dread. After years of managing Kubernetes clusters for side projects, I wanted something that got out of the way and let me focus on the work that actually matters. Fly.io promised exactly that: deploy a container, get a URL, move on with your life.

“The best infrastructure is the kind you stop thinking about entirely.”

The migration itself was surprisingly straightforward. A Dockerfile, a fly.toml configuration file, and a few CLI commands later, the site was live on edge servers distributed across the globe. No more YAML sprawl. No more debugging node affinity rules at midnight. Just code, deployed.
